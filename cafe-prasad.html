Our Story

Prasad — The Soul Behind Every Sip

With over 10 years of dedicated craftsmanship in the heart of Konijedu, Prasad has grown from a humble tea-stall enthusiast into one of the region's most respected Tea Masters and Coffee Artisans.

Every cup poured at Cafe Prasad is the result of deep experience — selecting the finest leaves, perfectly timing brews, and understanding the palate of each guest who walks through the door.

The Journey

10 Years, One Passion

  • 🌱
    2014 — The Beginning
    First Cup, First Dream

    Prasad began his journey at a small roadside stall in Konijedu, learning the art of chai brewing from local elders.

  • ☕
    2016 — Growing Skills
    Mastering South Indian Coffee

    Trained under experienced filter-coffee brewers, perfecting the decoction process and the art of the perfect frothy pour.

  • 🏆
    2019 — Recognition
    A Name in Konijedu

    Word spread — Prasad's masala chai became the talk of the town. Regulars queued up every morning for his signature brew.

  • 🏠
    2021 — Cafe Prasad Opens
    A Permanent Home

    The beloved brand became a proper cafe — a warm, welcoming space for locals to enjoy handcrafted drinks daily.

  • ⭐
    2024 — A Decade of Excellence
    10 Years Strong

    Cafe Prasad celebrates a decade of serving joy, one cup at a time — a true landmark of Konijedu's café culture.
